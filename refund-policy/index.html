Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Operator

SigFix is operated by Spendly (trading as Syntax Labs). All transactions are processed by Paddle.com as our Merchant of Record. Paddle handles payment processing, invoicing, and refund administration on our behalf.

Overview

SigFix offers two paid Pro plans with different refund rules. Please read the section that applies to your purchase:

  • Lifetime Pro — one-time payment of $14
  • Annual Pro — recurring subscription at $12/year

For Lifetime Pro (One-Time Payment)

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all Lifetime Pro purchases. If SigFix does not solve your Outlook signature problem, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your original purchase date.

To qualify for a refund:

  • Your request must be submitted within 14 calendar days of the purchase date.
  • You must provide the email address used at checkout or your Paddle order receipt.
  • Refunds are not available after the 14-day window has expired.

Because Lifetime Pro is a one-time payment with no recurring billing, no cancellation is required — a refund fully closes your purchase.

For Annual Pro (Subscription)

Annual Pro is a recurring subscription billed at $12 per year. Refund rules differ from Lifetime Pro:

Cancellation

Cancelling your subscription stops all future automatic renewals. You can cancel at any time via:

  • The subscription management link in your Paddle purchase or renewal email.
  • Paddle customer support (details in your receipt).
  • Emailing us at support@syntaxlabs.io.

After cancellation, your Pro access continues until the end of the current paid annual period. No further charges will be made.

Refunds for the Current Billing Period

Once an annual billing period has been paid, refunds for that period are generally not provided. You retain full Pro access for the remainder of the year you have already paid for, even after cancelling.

First Payment Refund Window

A refund is available for the initial Annual Pro subscription payment only, and only if requested within 14 days of the first purchase date. This applies exclusively to your first activation — not to subsequent automatic renewals.

Automatic renewal charges are non-refundable. If your subscription renews and you no longer wish to continue, cancel before the next renewal date to prevent future charges. The renewal payment itself cannot be refunded, but your access will remain active through the end of that paid year.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the eligible window, you may:

  • Contact Paddle customer support directly using the link or contact details in your purchase confirmation email.
  • Email us at support@syntaxlabs.io with your order details and we will facilitate the process with Paddle on your behalf.

Please include your purchase email address and, if available, your Paddle order or transaction ID to speed up processing.

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed by Paddle.com and typically appear in your account within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment method and financial institution. Paddle will send you a confirmation email once the refund has been initiated.

Exceptions

Refunds may be denied in the following circumstances:

  • The refund request falls outside the applicable 14-day window.
  • The request relates to an automatic subscription renewal (renewals are non-refundable).
  • Evidence of misuse, abuse, or violation of our Terms of Service.
  • A refund has already been issued for the same purchase.

Contact

For refund requests or billing questions, contact us at:

  • Email: support@syntaxlabs.io
  • Legal: legal@syntaxlabs.io

You may also contact Paddle customer support directly for billing and refund inquiries related to your order.
